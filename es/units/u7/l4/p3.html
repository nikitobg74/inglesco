🧠 GRAMÁTICA: There is y There are

Usamos there are para objetos en plural.

🔵 Singular — un objeto
There is a dog. 👉 Hay un perro. There is a book. 👉 Hay un libro.
🟢 Plural — más de un objeto
There are two dogs. 👉 Hay dos perros. There are two books. 👉 Hay dos libros.
